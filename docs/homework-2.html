  1. What ideas from Topics 4 and 5 (Causal Graphs as Statistical Models and Key Structures in Causal Graphs) would you categorize as an Enduring Concept, Important Concept, or a Concept Worth Being Familiar With? Why? (Refer to our syllabus for descriptions of these concept types.)

Try to use examples that are different from the ones in the pre-class concept videos. Your writing should be accessible to an audience who has not taken Probability.
